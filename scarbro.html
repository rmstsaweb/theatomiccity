The Scarboro 85 was a group of 85 black students who began learning in an all-white school. In 1955, the group of 85 black children from the Scarboro neighborhood began learning in Robertsville middle school and Oak Ridge High school. This was a huge push in the war against segregation. The Scarbro 85 are largely unrecognized, and many African American families are making the push to make their story hear worldwide.
